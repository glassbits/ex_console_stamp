Privacy Policy for Stamp Console.JS

Last updated: February 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Stamp Console.JS (the "Extension") handles your information. We are committed to respecting your privacy and protecting your data.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

All data created or processed by the Extension (such as your saved scripts, parameters, and settings) is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage.local API. This data never leaves your browser.

2. Permissions Usage

The Extension requests the following permissions for specific functionalities:

  • Storage: To save your scripts and preferences locally on your computer.
  • Scripting: To execute the JavaScript code you write on the web pages you visit.
  • Side Panel: To display the user interface in the browser side panel.
  • Host Permissions (All URLs): To allow the extension to function on any website you choose to run your scripts on. The extension only interacts with a page when you explicitly run a script.

3. Third-Party Services

This Extension does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Chrome Web Store support page.
